FAQ's


Can I use Netspend without activation?

No, your Netspend card cannot be used until it is activated. Activation links your card to your account and ensures it is secure for transactions. Without activation, the card will be inactive and unable to make purchases or withdraw funds.


Is Netspend a gift card?

No, Netspend cards are not gift cards. They are prepaid debit cards that offer greater flexibility. Unlike gift cards, Netspend cards can receive direct deposits, be reloaded with funds, and are not restricted to specific retailers. They function similarly to traditional debit cards but without requiring a bank account.


How do I check my Netspend card balance?

You can check your Netspend card balance through several methods:

  • Online Account Center: Log in to your Netspend account at ‘www.netspend.com/activate’ to view your balance and transaction history.
  • Mobile App: Download the Netspend app from the App Store or Google Play. Once logged in, you can check your balance and manage your account.
  • ATM: Use your Netspend card at an ATM to check your balance. Be aware of any fees that may apply for balance inquiries.
  • Customer Service: Call Netspend customer service at the number on the back of your card for balance information and assistance.

What should I do if I forget my Netspend PIN?

If you forget your PIN, you can reset it by logging into your Netspend account online or through the mobile app. Alternatively, contact Netspend customer service for assistance in resetting your PIN.


Are there any fees associated with using my Netspend card?

Yes, Netspend cards may have fees associated with various services, such as monthly maintenance fees, ATM withdrawal fees, and reloading fees. Review the Netspend cardholder agreement or visit the Netspend website for a detailed list of fees and terms.


Can I use my Netspend card internationally?

Yes, Netspend cards can be used internationally wherever Visa or Mastercard is accepted. However, be aware that foreign transaction fees may apply. Check the terms and conditions of your card for details on international usage and fees.
